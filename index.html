Problem Solving Tools

Break It Down

Take a big problem and split it into smaller parts you can solve one at a time.

Find the Root Cause

Ask “Why?” several times until you reach the real issue instead of the surface issue.

Focus on What Matters

Identify the one action that would make the biggest positive difference today.

Mental Models

80/20 Rule

A small number of actions often create most of the results. Find the highest-impact work.

First Principles

Strip away assumptions and rebuild your thinking from the most basic truths.

Second-Order Thinking

Think beyond the immediate result. Ask what happens next, and then after that.

Productivity Ideas

1 Big Goal

Choose one main goal each day before doing smaller tasks.

Deep Work

Work in focused blocks without distractions for stronger results.

Review and Reset
